Happy Math

Can you keep the mathematical expressions in your head and use them to select numbers for a short time? Yes? Well! Full ahead!

The idea of the game is simple. The player must keep up to click on the numbers that appear on the circle display for a limited period of time. He must do it in accordance with the conditions at the beginning of the level. Do you think it is easy? Not really. The player should consider different bonuses and penalties. In addition, numbers will change faster and conditions will become more difficult with each level.


  • Date: 01 Mar 2020
  • Platforms: Android, Web
  • Category: Logic game

Flappy Space

Towards to space adventures!

The game “Flappy Space” is an arcade in the space style, where you should try to overcome as many obstacles as possible on the space plate. The shields of the ship have three charges capable of protecting the ship from impacts with obstacles.


  • Date: 05 Jun 2017
  • Platforms: Android
  • Category: Space arcade

Checkers

Let's play?

It has two options, a game with a computer and a game between two real players. You can also change the style of the game board as you wish. Artificial intelligence is similar to the human mentality, the computer will fight fiercely, but seeing that it is losing, it will lose heart and begin to surrender to the player. Thank you for your attention! Good game!

About me

I am Andrew. I am a programmer. This is my story.

  • 2014-2015

    Start of programming

    In the beginning, I was fond of Minecraft. And suddenly the thought struck me to make a couple of mods for this game. So in 2014, I first became acquainted with programming.

  • 2015-2016

    My first game

    Later I wanted to make my own programs. At first it was very hard. I tried to make a 3d game, but nothing worked. Because of this, I almost quit programming. Later I decided to do something simpler. So the game "Checkers" appeared.

  • 2016-2017

    Android game development

    I decided to port the game "Checkers" to android. Then I released the android game "Flappy Space".

  • 2017-2020

    Search for yourself. Next turns

    There was a long pause in game development. I was engaged in web and application development. I wanted to try other areas of IT. But in the autumn of 2019, I returned to game development and released the game "Happy Math" in 2020. Now I am planning to create the game "Black Fox" and the program "Famous people".
